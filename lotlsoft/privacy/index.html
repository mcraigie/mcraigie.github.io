Lotlsoft Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you install and use Piksi (the “App”).

PERSONAL INFORMATION WE COLLECT

When you use the App, we automatically collect certain information about your device, IP address, and time zone. Additionally, as you use the App, we collect information about the individual screens you view, and information about how you interact with the App. We refer to this automatically-collected information as “Analytics.” When we talk about “Personal Information” in this Privacy Policy, we are talking about this Analytics information.

HOW DO WE USE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?

We use the Analytics that we collect to improve and optimize our App.

SHARING YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION

We share your Personal Information with third parties to help us use your Personal Information, as described above. We use Google Firebase to help us understand how our customers use the App--you can read more about how Google uses your Personal Information here: https://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/privacy/. Finally, we may also share your Personal Information to comply with applicable laws and regulations, to respond to a subpoena, search warrant or other lawful request for information we receive, or to otherwise protect our rights.

CHANGES

We may update this privacy policy from time to time in order to reflect, for example, changes to our practices or for other operational, legal or regulatory reasons.
